ABC Tune Explorer

User Manual

1. Search & Navigation

The interface is designed to provide intuitive access to thousands of traditional melodies.

Search Fields

  • General Search: Search by (part of) the title, composer, notes, transcription, group, history, or source. Wildcards are not needed; the system searches for "contains" by default. You can also search by Tune ID (e.g., 77277) directly.

Specific Filters

  • Key: Filter by key. Use standard notation like G, Am, Dmix.
  • Mode: Filter by musical mode (e.g., Mixolydian, Dorian).
  • Rhythm: Filter by dance type or meter, such as Reel, Jig, Waltz.
  • Composer: Search for tunes by or attributed to a specific person.
  • Favorites: Show only tunes you have marked with a heart.

2. Melody Details View

Click "View melody" to open the details. Here you can read, listen to, and analyze the music.

Audio & Display

  • Play: Use the Play/Pause button to listen.
  • Instrument: Choose from Piano, Violin, Flute, or Accordion.
  • Tempo: Adjust the playback speed without changing the pitch.
  • Transpose: Use the +1/-1 buttons to raise or lower the melody in real-time. Both sheet music and audio adapt instantly.

Export

  • ABC: Download the source code.
  • MIDI: Download the audio file.
  • PDF: Download the sheet music for printing.

3. Advanced Features

Find Similar Tunes

Click this button at the bottom of the details to find variations. The system analyzes musical intervals.

  • 0.0: Exact match.
  • < 10.0: Likely a close variation.
Tip: Your favorites are safely stored in our database, linked to your browser. You won't lose them if you refresh the page!
